Naná Vasconcelos

One of the most revered and influential percussionists of all time

Born in Recife, Brazil, Juvenal de Holanda Vasconcelos was regarded as one of the greatest percussionists of all time. Eight-time Grammy winner, his works include collaborations with musicians such as Pat Metheny, Arto Lindsay, Jon Hassell, Milton Nascimento and many others. ‘Naná’ died from lung cancer in 2016 at the age of 71.
